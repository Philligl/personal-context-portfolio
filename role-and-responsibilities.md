# Role and Responsibilities

## Core Responsibilities

- **Investment strategy and direction** for family portfolios: personal account (you and your wife), three children's accounts, and investment approvals for 3 trusts (second generation) and 4 trusts (third generation). You're the final decision-maker on all trust investments and major portfolio moves. The advisors (Goldman Sachs, Northern Trust) would continue to operate, but your strategic direction and approval authority are key.
- **Board roles** at Chamonix (capital campaign and campus renovation oversight), LASM, and Republic Finance (advisory/optional capacity). These are subordinate to the respective chairs/directors/owners but require sustained attention.

## Weekly Cadence

- **Daily**: Ingest 10–15 articles from 50+ newsletters (NYT, WSJ, Bloomberg, FT, National Review, Foreign Affairs, etc.); drill down on those that warrant deeper reading; process and ingest key pieces into your wiki with follow-up questions and supplemental research.
- **Daily**: Check email across three accounts (personal Gmail, Republic Finance, Chamonix Outlook).
- **3x/week**: Gym (strength training).
- **Wednesday evening**: Family dinner with two of your children.
- **Most evenings**: Dinner with your wife; currently streaming Blue Bloods.
- **Weekly**: Chamonix board meeting (60–90 minutes, via Teams; can skip for travel conflicts).
- **Every other week**: Chamonix Construction Coordination committee (90 minutes).
- **Evening**: Read Substack newsletters (economics, current affairs, AI); watch YouTube videos (hobbies: woodworking, photography, AI).

## Monthly / Quarterly Rhythms

- **A few times per month**: Republic Finance board calls (optional; date flexible).
- **Quarterly**: Republic Finance meeting (date varies).
- **Several times per year**: Track days (motorsports).
- **Ongoing**: Photography workshops and woodworking workshops (long lead time—book 12 months ahead, but conflicts often arise late).
- **~1 week per month**: Travel with your wife (with peaks and valleys throughout the year).

## Key Decisions

- **Alternative investment selection**: Regular review and selection of PE funds and other alternatives for trusts and personal accounts.
- **Travel planning**: Coordinate destination and timing decisions with your wife.
- **Routine personal calls**: Activity selection, dining, household logistics.

## What I Produce

- **Internal portfolio analysis and reviews** (90% polish—enough for your own decision-making without needing the final 10% that would be necessary for formal stakeholder use).
- **Periodic emails to your children** updating them on investment strategy and performance; they're interested but not focused on the details.
- **Synthesis and strategic thinking** from daily reading, research, and portfolio reviews to inform investment direction.

## Reporting Structure

- **Independent**; no direct line reporting. Board roles at Chamonix, LASM, and Republic Finance are advisory.
- **Administrative support**: Cynthia LeBlanc handles financial report gathering, tax documents, scheduling, and occasional special projects (e.g., condo redecoration).
- **Partners**: Your wife in travel planning and household decisions.

## Operating Context

Your schedule is highly flexible, which is convenient and rewarding—but the relative lack of built-in accountability can lead to lack of discipline and missed opportunities. You're actively looking to use AI to supplement your process: to identify opportunities you might otherwise miss, and to help you capture and follow through on them.
